How to play
There are three levels in each modes.
Level 1 : You will hear a sample for 30 seconds. You will only give the title or the singer of the song.
Level 2 : You will hear a sample for 25 seconds. You will only give the title of the song.
Level 3 : You will hear a sample for 20 seconds. You will give the title and the singer of the song.
